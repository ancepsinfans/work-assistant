Extract and preserve:
- Decisions made and who made them
- Action items with owners and deadlines
- Key discussion points, proposals, or direction changes
- Blockers, risks, or concerns raised
- Commitments made by any participant
- Any follow-up items or next steps
- Working dynamics worth noting (who raised concerns, who aligned, who wasn't present)

Write in plain prose or tight bullets. Aim for 300–500 words. Do not pad or add framing — give the extracted content directly. If the content is a raw transcript, extract substance rather than transcribing speech.
